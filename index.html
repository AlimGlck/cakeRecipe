Harry Potter Birthday Cake

  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Cake's Story

This cake was brought by Hagrid to Harry for his eleventh birthday. It was a little bit crushed because Hagrid sat on it. That was Harry's first birthday cake ever in his life. It was made by Hagrid himself.

Ingredients

  • For Sponge Cake:
    • Six eggs
    • A glass of sugar
    • Half a glass of hot water
    • A glass of flour
    • A glass of starch
    • A glass of cocoa
    • Four-five drops extract of vanilla
    • A pack of baking powder
  • For Cream:
    • One and half pack of cream
    • Three-four drops extract of vanilla
    • One and half tablespoons of sugar
    • Two-three drops food coloring
  • For Middle Layer:
    • A banana
  • For the Above:
    • A tube of glaze

Directions

  1. First of all, for the sponge cake, whisk the eggs and sugar until it reaches the consistency of mayonnaise. Let's not forget to add half a tea glass of hot water while whisking so that the sponge cake is like a sponge.
  2. Let's sift A glass of flour, A glass of starch and half a glass of cocoa to our mixture that has the consistency of mayonnaise.
  3. After adding the baking soda and vanilla extract, whisk the mixture with the low setting of the mixer until the mixture becomes liquid.
  4. Then, let's lay baking paper under the 20 cm springform cake mold and pour our mixture. Bake in a preheated 200 degree oven for 30 minutes. (cook with control)
  5. After the sponge cake is cooked, let it cool.
  6. In the meantime, let's whisk our 1.5 packets of cream, 1.5 tablespoons of sugar, 3-4 drops of vanilla extract and the material we will use for the pink color with a mixer, with the help of a mixer.
  7. Then let's divide our sponge cake into 2 equal parts. If our sponge cake is swollen, let's cut it with the help of a knife and make it flat.
  8. Let's pour some of our cream on the middle floor and line up the bananas we cut and close the other sponge cake. Let's eat our remaining cream on the sides and top of the cake. It doesn't need to be very straight.
  9. Then, let's make an inverted letter S on the cake with the help of a knife, from top to bottom.
  10. Let's draw a sideways line from the curved letter S. Let's write the text we want with green edible tube glaze as in the photo. You can stay true to the original like me. Bon Appetit!
